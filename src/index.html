Game Instructions

  • The fox only have one live, you have to take care of him very well.
  • You have three buttons at the bottom, the side ones help you to move between the actions and the middle one is to perform an action
  • Action 1: Feed the fox.
    Action 2: Clean fox's poop.
    Action 3: Change the weather.
  • When the fox opens his mouth you have to feed him, if not after a random time he is going to die.
  • After fox pooping, you have to clean it up, if not the enviroment will be dirty and the fox is going to die.
  • On nights, you don't have to be aware of anything.

Keep your fox alive!
